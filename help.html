Orders & Delivery
How do I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an email with tracking details. You can also check real-time status under My Orders → Order ID → Track.
What is the delivery timeline?
Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days. Metro cities usually receive orders within 2–3 days. Delivery timelines may vary based on your location and seller.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, you can cancel an order before it is shipped. Go to My Orders, click on the order, and select "Cancel Order." Once shipped, cancellation is not possible.
Returns & Refunds
What is the return policy?
Most products have a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. To be eligible, the product must be unused, in original packaging, and include all accessories.
How long do refunds take?
Refunds are initiated within 24 hours of return pickup and typically reflect in your account within 5–7 business days depending on your bank or payment method.
Account & Payments
Is my payment information secure?
Yes, all transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your full card details on our servers. All payments are processed through certified payment gateways.
How do I reset my password?
On the login page, click "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email and you'll receive an OTP to reset your password securely.
How do I change my email address?
Email address cannot be changed after registration as it is linked to your account identity. If you need help, please contact our support team.
